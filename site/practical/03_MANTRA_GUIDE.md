### 5.1 Basic Practice

```
PREPARATION:
1. Clean yourself (at least hands, face)
2. Sit facing East or North
3. Spine straight, comfortable
4. Set intention for the practice

PRACTICE:
1. Take 3 deep breaths
2. Invoke your Guru/Divine (optional)
3. Begin mantra:
   â€¢ First 3 times aloud (Vaikhari)
```

## 6. Common Mistakes {#6-mistakes}

| Mistake | Problem | Solution |
|---------|---------|----------|
| **Racing** | No benefit, just counting | Slow down, feel each syllable |
| **Mispronunciation** | Wrong frequency | Learn correct pronunciation |
| **No feeling** | Mechanical | Add devotion/intention |
| **Inconsistency** | Samskaras don't form | Daily practice, same mantra |
| **Too many mantras** | Energy scattered | Stick to one main mantra |
| **Expecting magic** | Disappointment | Trust the process, be patient |
