## Changing Your Guna Mix

You can consciously shift your composition:

### To Increase Sattva (Clarity)
- Meditation
- Pure food (fresh, vegetarian)
- Nature
- Morning sunlight
- Truthfulness

### To Decrease Tamas (Inertia)
- Exercise
- Cold showers
- Fasting
- Early rising
- Less sleep

### To Balance Rajas (Activity)
- Breathwork
- Purposeful action (not frantic)
- Service to others
- Completing tasks
