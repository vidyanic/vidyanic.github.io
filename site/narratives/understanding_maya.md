## üé¨ What Is Maya?

Maya is NOT saying "reality is fake."

Maya is saying: **"Reality isn't what you THINK it is."**

Think of it this way:

| What You See | What It Is |
|--------------|-----------|
| A rainbow | Light refraction |
| A mirage | Heat waves |
| A movie | Projected light |
| Reality (as experienced) | Maya's rendering |

Maya is the "graphics engine" of reality.

Right now, you're reading this on a screen.

**On the screen:**

## üîÆ Why Does Maya Exist?

Good question! Several perspectives:

### 1. The Game Needs a Screen
You can't play without graphics. Maya renders the game you experience.

### 2. Brahman Experiences Itself
The One becomes Many to know itself. Maya is how this multiplicity appears.

### 3. It's Part of the Design
Like dreams serve the dreamer, Maya serves consciousness exploring itself.

## üß© Maya's Three Qualities (Gunas)

Maya operates through three forces:

| Guna | Quality | Maya Effect |
|------|---------|-------------|
| **Sattva** | Clarity | Shows truth partially |
| **Rajas** | Activity | Creates distraction, desire |
| **Tamas** | Inertia | Creates ignorance, sleep |

Most of us are caught in Rajas-Tamas: busy and confused.

Sattva helps us see through Maya.

## ‚ùì Common Questions

### "If it's illusion, why bother?"

Because even in the game, there are better and worse ways to play. And the game ends when you truly see through it.

### "Does Maya deceive me?"

Maya doesn't intend to deceive. You deceive yourself by taking the appearance as ultimate.

### "Can I function if I see through Maya?"

Absolutely. You function BETTER. Like an actor who knows it's a play performs more freely.
