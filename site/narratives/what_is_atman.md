## üß™ A Simple Experiment

Try this right now:

1. **Close your eyes** for 10 seconds
2. **Watch your thoughts** come and go
3. Now ask: **Who is watching the thoughts?**

The thoughts change. The emotions change. But the **watcher** never changes.

That unchanging witness is Atman.

## ‚ùì Common Questions

No. It's direct experience. Every meditation tradition aims to help you experience this directly.
