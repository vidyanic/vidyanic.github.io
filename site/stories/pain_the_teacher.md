## üßò The Practice For Right Now

If you're in acute pain, try this:

### 1. Stop Resisting

Not "like it." Not "want it." Just... stop fighting it.

The resistance is what makes suffering unbearable. The pain underneath is just... pain.

### 2. Breathe Into It

Literally breathe into the physical sensation. Expand around it. Give it space.

The pain is here. The space you give it determines whether it stays or moves.

### 3. Name The Sensation, Not The Story

"There's pressure in my chest" ‚Äî yes.
"My life is ruined" ‚Äî that's story.

Stay with sensation. Story multiplies suffering.

### 4. Ask: "Who Is Aware Of This?"

Even now. Even in this. There's an awareness.

**That awareness is what you are. And it's not in pain.**

### 5. Trust The Process

Not blindly. But knowingly.

This is curriculum. This is burning karma. This is liberation disguised as destruction.

**The caterpillar doesn't know it's becoming a butterfly. It just feels like dying.**
