## 1.1 The Power of Sound

Sound surrounds us. It heals us. It disturbs us. Music can make us weep or dance. A mother's voice calms an infant. Thunder evokes primal fear. But why? What makes sound so powerful?

Modern physics treats sound as mechanical waves — useful for communication, but secondary to electromagnetic phenomena. Yet ancient traditions worldwide place sound at the foundation of reality:

- **Vedas:** "In the beginning was Nada (नाद/sound)"
- **Bible:** "In the beginning was the Word"
- **Kabbalah:** Creation through divine speech
- **Buddhism:** Dharma arises from sound

This paper explores whether these traditions describe something fundamental about reality.

---

## 1.2 The Healing Question

Sound therapy is experiencing a renaissance:
- Music therapy reduces pain by 30-40%
- Tibetan bowls decrease stress markers
- Binaural beats enhance meditation
- Specific frequencies show biological effects

Yet we lack a unified theory explaining:
1. WHY does sound heal?
2. WHY do specific frequencies have specific effects?
3. HOW does sound interact with cells and consciousness?
