# 4. HYPOTHESIS

---

## 4.1 Primary Hypothesis

**H1: Sound is foundational to reality (Shabda-Brahman), and healing through sound works by restoring correct vibrational frequencies at cellular and systemic levels.**

---

## 4.2 Sub-Hypotheses

### H1a: Sound Creates Form

Sound frequencies create and maintain physical forms. Matter is crystallized vibration.

**Predictions:**
- Cymatics demonstrates sound -> pattern
- Cells respond to specific frequencies
- Structural changes from sound exposure

### H1b: Specific Frequencies Have Specific Effects

Different frequencies produce different biological and psychological effects due to resonance.

**Predictions:**
- 528 Hz affects DNA differently than 440 Hz
- Chakra frequencies correlate with body regions
- Therapeutic frequencies can be identified

### H1c: Healing Is Frequency Restoration

Disease involves frequency disruption; healing involves restoration of correct frequencies.

**Predictions:**
- Diseased tissue has altered frequency
- Correct frequency application heals
- Coherence restoration improves health

### H1d: Consciousness Responds to Sound

Sound directly affects consciousness states through vibrational resonance.

**Predictions:**
- Binaural beats alter brain states
- Mantra produces specific effects
- Music changes consciousness measurably

---

## 4.3 Null Hypotheses

### H0a: Sound Is Secondary

Sound is merely mechanical waves in matter, with no foundational role.

**If true:**
- No pattern creation from sound (falsified by cymatics)
- No frequency-specific cellular effects
- Sound healing is placebo only

### H0b: Effects Are Non-Specific

All sound effects are due to relaxation, not frequency-specific.

**If true:**
- Any sound would work equally
- No difference between frequencies
- Specific frequencies are irrelevant
