## 4.3 Null Hypotheses

### H₀₁: Random Cycles

Cosmic cycles are random, unrelated phenomena with no underlying pattern.

**Against:** The precise mathematical ratios (4:3:2:1) suggest intentional design.

### H₀₂: Pure Physics

All cycles are fully explained by gravitational/mechanical physics alone.

**Against:** The correlation with consciousness and societal patterns exceeds mechanical causation.

### H₀₃: No Fractal Pattern

Cycles at different scales are independent and non-self-similar.

**Against:** The same Create-Maintain-Destroy pattern appears at all scales.
