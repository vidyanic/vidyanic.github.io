## 3.2 Yuga Mechanics

### 3.2.1 The Four Yugas

```
YUGA ARCHITECTURE:
==================

SATYA YUGA (सत्य युग)
Duration: 1,728,000 years
Dharma: 100% (4 legs/पाद)
Guna: Sattva dominant
Features: Truth prevalent, telepathy, long lives

TRETA YUGA (त्रेता युग)
Duration: 1,296,000 years
Dharma: 75% (3 legs)
Guna: Sattva + emerging Rajas
Features: Ritual begins, some decline

DWAPARA YUGA (द्वापर युग)
Duration: 864,000 years
Dharma: 50% (2 legs)
Guna: Rajas dominant
Features: Further decline, memory loss

KALI YUGA (कलि युग)
Duration: 432,000 years
Dharma: 25% (1 leg)
Guna: Tamas dominant
Features: Maximum Maya, truth hidden
```

### 3.2.2 The 4:3:2:1 Ratio

The Yuga durations follow a strict mathematical ratio:

```
Satya : Treta : Dwapara : Kali = 4 : 3 : 2 : 1

MATHEMATICAL FORMULA:
Satya = 4 × 432,000 = 1,728,000 years
Treta = 3 × 432,000 = 1,296,000 years
Dwapara = 2 × 432,000 = 864,000 years
Kali = 1 × 432,000 = 432,000 years

TOTAL = 10 × 432,000 = 4,320,000 years (1 Mahayuga)
```

## 3.3 Sandhya (Transition) Periods

### 3.3.1 Dawn and Dusk

Each Yuga has transition periods:

```
SANDHYA STRUCTURE:
==================

Each Yuga = Core Period + Sandhya (dawn) + Sandhyamsha (dusk)

Transition = 10% of Yuga duration

Example for Kali Yuga:
Core: 388,800 years
Sandhya: 21,600 years
Sandhyamsha: 21,600 years
Total: 432,000 years
```

### 3.3.2 Transition Characteristics

During Sandhya periods:
- Old rules decay, new rules not fully active
- Maximum chaos and confusion
- Rapid karmic manifestation
- More "glitches" visible
- Accelerated awakening for prepared individuals

## 3.4 Kalpa Architecture

### 3.4.1 Higher Cycle Nesting

```
BRAHMA LIFESPAN:
================

1 Brahma Lifespan = 100 Brahma Years
1 Brahma Year = 360 Brahma Days
1 Brahma Day (Kalpa) = 4.32 billion years
1 Brahma Night = 4.32 billion years (dormancy)

WITHIN 1 KALPA:
14 Manvantaras
Each Manvantara = 71.428 Mahayugas
Each Mahayuga = 4.32 million years
```

### 3.4.2 Universal Reset Mechanics

At the end of each Kalpa:
- Partial Pralaya (dissolution)
- Information preserved in causal realm
- New creation cycle begins
- Pattern repeats with variations
