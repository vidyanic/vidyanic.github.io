# 4. Hypothesis

## 4.1 Primary Hypothesis

> **H₀: Mass is Tamas concentration, and gravity is the expression of Tamas through spacetime geometry.**

This claims:
- Mass and Tamas are the same phenomenon
- Gravitational attraction is Tamas-induced geodesic following
- Inertia and gravity are both Tamas manifestations
- The equivalence principle is automatic (not mysterious)

---

## 4.2 Sub-Hypotheses

### H1: Mass = Tamas

> **H1: Mass is the physical measurement of Tamas concentration: M ∝ T**

**Rationale:**
- Both measure heaviness
- Both resist change (inertia)
- Both bind/attract

**Testable Prediction:**
High-density (heavy) matter has Tamas-dominant Guna composition.

---

### H2: Equivalence is Identity

> **H2: Gravitational mass equals inertial mass because both ARE Tamas.**

**Rationale:**
- Gravitational mass = Tamas (source of gravity)
- Inertial mass = Tamas (resistance to change)
- Same phenomenon → Same measurement

**Testable Prediction:**
No deviation from equivalence principle will ever be found.

---

### H3: Curvature = Tamas Density

> **H3: Spacetime curvature is proportional to Tamas concentration.**

**Rationale:**
- Einstein: Mass-energy curves spacetime
- Backend: Tamas is mass-energy
- Therefore: Tamas curves spacetime

**Testable Prediction:**
All gravitational phenomena consistent with Tamas field model.

---

### H4: Dark Matter = Multi-Loka Tamas

> **H4: Dark matter is Tamas in adjacent Lokas that affects our spacetime but is invisible to our senses.**

**Rationale:**
- 14 Lokas exist (Backend)
- We see only Bhuloka
- Tamas in other Lokas still has mass
- Explains "missing" gravitational effects

**Testable Prediction:**
Dark matter distribution matches multi-Loka Tamas model better than particle models.

---

## 4.3 Null Hypothesis

> **H_null: Mass is unrelated to Tamas, and gravity is simply a fundamental force with no deeper explanation.**

**Requirements to Accept Null:**
1. Mass property that contradicts Tamas definition
2. Deviation from equivalence principle
3. Gravity without mass (counterexample)
4. Mass without inertia (counterexample)

**Current Status:**
No evidence supports the null hypothesis. All properties correspond.

---

## 4.4 Falsifiability

### What Would Disprove Our Hypothesis:

| Prediction | Falsification Condition |
|------------|------------------------|
| H1 | Mass without heaviness/inertia properties |
| H2 | Gravitational mass ≠ Inertial mass |
| H3 | Curvature without mass-energy |
| H4 | Dark matter detected as particles |
| H₀ | Any of the above |

### Specific Falsifiable Claims:

1. **If** gravitational mass differs from inertial mass → **H2 falsified**
2. **If** massless objects gravitate → **H1 challenged**
3. **If** dark matter particles are found → **H4 alternative confirmed**
4. **If** Tamas-reducing practices don't affect weight → **H1 weakened**
