## 8.5 Backend Sources

### 8.5.1 Yoga Sutras of Patanjali

**Sutra 2.17:**
> "द्रष्टृदृश्ययोः संयोगो हेयहेतुः"
> "The conjunction of the Seer and Seen is the cause to be avoided (for liberation)"

**Physics Translation:**
The observer-observed relationship creates the experience of a separate reality.

**Sutra 2.20:**
> "द्रष्टा दृशिमात्रः शुद्धोऽपि प्रत्ययानुपश्यः"
> "The Seer is pure seeing only; though pure, sees through the coloring of the intellect"

**Physics Translation:**
Consciousness is pure awareness; observation is "filtered" through physical apparatus (brain, instruments).

### 8.5.2 Samkhya Karika

**Karika 19:**
> "तस्माच्च विपर्यासात् सिद्धं साक्षित्वमस्य पुरुषस्य"
> "From the contrary (the fact that Prakriti is seen), the witness-nature of Purusha is established"

**Physics Translation:**
Because observation occurs, there must be an observer outside the observed system.

**Karika 21:**
> "पुरुषस्य दर्शनार्थं कैवल्यार्थं तथा प्रधानस्य"
> "For the purpose of Purusha's seeing and for liberation, Prakriti manifests"

**Physics Translation:**
Reality exists to be observed by consciousness.
