### 10.1.1 Frequency-Guna Relationship

```
f_loka = f₀ × (S/T)^α

WHERE:
f₀ = Bhu-Loka reference frequency
S = Sattva proportion (0-1)
T = Tamas proportion (0-1)
α = 2 (scaling exponent)

EXAMPLE:
Satya-Loka: S=0.99, T=0.005
f_satya = f₀ × (0.99/0.005)² = f₀ × 39,204 ≈ f₀ × 10⁴·⁵
```

### 10.1.2 Energy Distribution

```
E_loka = E_total × (f_loka / Σf)

WHERE:
E_total = Total cosmic energy
f_loka = Frequency of that Loka
Σf = Sum of all 14 Loka frequencies
```

### 10.1.3 Time Dilation per Loka

```
t_loka = t_bhu × (f₀/f_loka)

Higher frequency -> Faster time (more ticks per Bhu second)
Lower frequency -> Slower time (fewer ticks per Bhu second)
```
