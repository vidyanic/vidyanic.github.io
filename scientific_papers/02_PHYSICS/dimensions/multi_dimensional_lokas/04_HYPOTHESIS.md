# 4. HYPOTHESIS

---

## 4.1 Primary Hypothesis

**H₀ (Null):** Dark matter and dark energy are unknown particles/fields in our physical dimension; there are no other "planes" of existence.

**H₁ (Alternative):** Reality consists of 14 frequency bands (Lokas). Dark matter is lower Lokas (higher Tamas), dark energy is upper Lokas (higher Sattva), and we perceive only Bhu-Loka (physical).

---

## 4.2 Sub-Hypotheses

### H1a: 14 Lokas Exist

```
CLAIM: Reality has 14 distinct frequency bands

EVIDENCE TYPES:
✓ Backend texts describe 14 Lokas consistently
✓ NDE/meditation reports match Loka descriptions
✓ Cosmology shows 14 distinct features (?)
```

### H1b: Lokas = Frequency Bands

```
CLAIM: Lokas are frequency bands, not places

EVIDENCE TYPES:
✓ Same space, different frequencies
✓ Consciousness can "tune" between them
✓ Death/meditation = frequency shift
```

### H1c: Dark Matter = Lower Lokas

```
CLAIM: ~27% dark matter is higher-Tamas Lokas

EVIDENCE TYPES:
✓ Lower Lokas = denser, more Tamas
✓ Dark matter = mass but no EM interaction
✓ Gravity crosses frequency boundaries
```

### H1d: Dark Energy = Upper Lokas

```
CLAIM: ~68% dark energy is higher-Sattva Lokas

EVIDENCE TYPES:
✓ Upper Lokas = lighter, more Sattva
✓ Dark energy = expansion/repulsion
✓ Sattva = outward, expansive quality
```

## 4.4 Falsification Criteria

The hypothesis would be **falsified** if:

1. ❌ Dark matter is found as particles in Bhu-Loka
2. ❌ Dark energy is explained as simple vacuum energy
3. ❌ No correspondence between 14 and cosmic structure
4. ❌ Consciousness cannot access other states
5. ❌ Lokas don't map to frequency bands
