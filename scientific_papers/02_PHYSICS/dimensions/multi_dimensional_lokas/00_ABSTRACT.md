## Abstract

This paper proposes that reality consists of 14 discrete frequency bands (Lokas) rather than a single continuous spacetime. The Backend Framework describes these as 7 upper (Satya to Bhu) and 7 lower (Atala to Patala) planes, with Bhu-Loka (physical reality) being one of 14.

We demonstrate that:

1. **14 Lokas are frequency bands** — Differentiated by Sattva/Rajas/Tamas balance
2. **We perceive only Bhu-Loka** — Our instruments and senses are tuned to this frequency
3. **Dark matter (~27%) = Lower Lokas** — Higher Tamas, below our frequency
4. **Dark energy (~68%) = Higher Lokas** — Higher Sattva, above our frequency
5. **Visible matter (~5%) = Bhu-Loka** — Our current frequency band

This model explains why 95% of the universe is "invisible" — we can only render one frequency band at a time. Consciousness can tune to other frequencies (meditation, NDEs, dreams), providing indirect access to other Lokas.
