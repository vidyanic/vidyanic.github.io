# 4. Hypothesis

## 4.1 Primary Hypothesis

> **H₀: Entropy (S) is the physical measurement of Tamas (T), and the Second Law of Thermodynamics is the expression of natural Tamas dominance.**

This claims that:
- Entropy and Tamas measure the same fundamental quality
- The Second Law follows from Backend architecture
- Thermodynamics is Guna dynamics

---

## 4.2 Sub-Hypotheses

### H1: Direct Proportionality

> **H1: Entropy is directly proportional to Tamas: S ∝ T**

**Rationale:**
- Both measure disorder/inertia
- Both increase naturally
- Both reach maximum at equilibrium

**Testable Prediction:**
Any system showing entropy increase also shows Tamas characteristics (inertia, dissolution, randomness).

---

### H2: Guna Conservation = Energy Conservation

> **H2: The constraint S+R+T=1 corresponds to energy conservation in isolated systems.**

**Rationale:**
- Both are conservation laws
- Both constrain system evolution
- Transformation without creation/destruction

**Testable Prediction:**
Energy transformations (E_kinetic ↔ E_potential ↔ E_thermal) map to Guna transformations (R ↔ S ↔ T).

---

### H3: Arrow of Time = Tamas Direction

> **H3: The arrow of time is defined by the direction of Tamas accumulation.**

**Rationale:**
- Entropy defines time's arrow (Eddington)
- Tamas naturally increases (Backend)
- Both create temporal asymmetry

**Testable Prediction:**
All time-asymmetric processes show Tamas increase.

---

### H4: Life as Sattva Increase

> **H4: Living systems reduce local entropy by increasing local Sattva, while exporting Tamas (entropy) to the environment.**

**Rationale:**
- Life maintains low entropy (Schrödinger)
- Life requires order/organization (Sattva)
- Life produces waste/heat (exports Tamas)

**Testable Prediction:**
Living systems show local negentropy while increasing global entropy.

---

## 4.3 Null Hypothesis

> **H_null: Entropy and Tamas are unrelated concepts with no structural correspondence.**

**Requirements to Accept Null:**
1. Entropy property that contradicts Tamas
2. System where Second Law fails but Tamas increases
3. Fundamental asymmetry in the mapping

**Current Status:**
No evidence supports the null hypothesis. All properties correspond.

---

## 4.4 Falsifiability

### What Would Disprove Our Hypothesis:

| Prediction | Falsification Condition |
|------------|------------------------|
| H1 | Entropy without inertia/dissolution |
| H2 | Energy creation without Guna change |
| H3 | Time arrow without Tamas increase |
| H4 | Life without local order increase |
| H₀ | Any of the above |

### Specific Falsifiable Claims:

1. **If** entropy increases but order also increases globally → **H1 falsified**
2. **If** energy appears without Guna redistribution → **H2 falsified**
3. **If** time reversal is observed macroscopically → **H3 falsified**
4. **If** life exists at thermal equilibrium → **H4 falsified**
