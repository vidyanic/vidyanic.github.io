# 4. Hypothesis

---

## 4.1 Central Hypothesis

**H₁:** Cancer is fundamentally a disease of lost cellular identity — dedifferentiation leading to primitive, survival-focused behavior.

---

## 4.2 Predictions

| Prediction | Test | Status |
|------------|------|--------|
| Cancer cells lose differentiation markers | Histology | ✓ Confirmed |
| Cancer metabolism is primitive | Warburg effect | ✓ Confirmed |
| Epigenetic marks disrupted | Methylation studies | ✓ Confirmed |
| Forcing differentiation can treat | Differentiation therapy | ✓ Some success |
