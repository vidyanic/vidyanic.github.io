## 1.2 THE PARADOX

The immune system evolved over billions of years to protect the body. It has sophisticated mechanisms to distinguish "self" from "non-self."

```
THE FUNDAMENTAL QUESTION:
=========================

How does a system designed for protection
make such a fundamental error as attacking self?

The immune system's PRIMARY function is to:
• Recognize self (allow)
• Recognize non-self (destroy)

In autoimmunity:
• Self is recognized as non-self
• Protection becomes destruction
```
