## 9.3 FOR SPECIFIC CONDITIONS

### Hashimoto's Thyroiditis

**Root cause often includes:**
1. Molecular mimicry — Gluten resembles thyroid tissue
2. Gut permeability — Allows gluten fragments into blood
3. Iodine imbalance — Alters thyroid markers
4. Stress — Increases TPO antibodies

**Protocol:**
- Remove gluten
- Heal gut
- Optimize iodine/selenium
- Manage stress
- Support regeneration

**Documented outcome:** 50-70% antibody reduction in responders

### Rheumatoid Arthritis

**Root cause often includes:**
1. Gut-joint axis disruption
2. Bacterial mimicry (Proteus, others)
3. Chronic inflammation
4. Stress-driven flares

**Protocol:**
- AIP diet
- Gut healing
- Anti-inflammatory support
- Stress management

### Multiple Sclerosis

**Root cause often includes:**
1. EBV molecular mimicry
2. Vitamin D deficiency (marker support)
3. Gut-brain axis disruption
4. Chronic stress

**Protocol:**
- Wahls Protocol or similar
- Vitamin D optimization
- Gut healing
- Mind-body practices
