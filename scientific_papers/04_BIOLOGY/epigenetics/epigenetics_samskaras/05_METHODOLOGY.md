| Category | Data Type | Sources |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| Basic epigenetics | Molecular mechanism | Textbooks, reviews |
| Transgenerational | Cohort studies | Dutch Hunger, Holocaust |
| Practice effects | Intervention studies | Meditation research |
| Backend | Philosophical texts | Yoga Sutras, Vedanta |

---

## 5.3 Evaluation Criteria

For correspondence to be valid:
- Structural match (overlay model)
- Functional match (shaping expression)
- Inheritance match (transmission)
- Plasticity match (modifiability)
