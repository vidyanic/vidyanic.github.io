## 1. Introduction

In 1953, Watson and Crick published the structure of DNA:
- Two polynucleotide strands
- Wound around each other in a right-handed helix

- Antiparallel orientation (strands run in opposite directions)
- ~10 base pairs per complete turn

This structure became the foundation of molecular biology.

- **DNA methylation** ‚Äî Adding methyl groups to DNA

- **Non-coding RNA** ‚Äî Regulatory molecules

- Acquired during life
- Influenced by environment, behavior, trauma
- Passed to offspring (transgenerational)
