# 4. HYPOTHESIS

---

## 4.1 Primary Hypothesis

**H1: Biological evolution is Jiva Yatra — the purposeful journey of consciousness through 8.4 million species, driven by Karma and Dharma, culminating in human birth where liberation becomes possible.**

---

## 4.2 Sub-Hypotheses

### H1a: Consciousness is Primary

Consciousness does not emerge from matter but pre-exists and expresses through increasingly complex forms.

**Predictions:**
- Hard problem remains unsolved by materialism (verified)
- Consciousness appears at all levels (panpsychism gaining support)
- Forms evolve to express consciousness (verified pattern)

### H1b: Evolution is Directed

Evolution shows consistent direction toward complexity and consciousness, not random drift.

**Predictions:**
- Complexity increases over time (verified)
- Convergent evolution shows constraints (verified)
- Consciousness increases with complexity (verified)

### H1c: Karma Determines Form

The specific form an individual Jiva takes is determined by Karma (causal history), not random chance.

**Predictions:**
- Past-life memories sometimes match species (some evidence)
- Behavioral tendencies carry over (Vasanas)
- Not truly random distribution

### H1d: Human is Unique Gateway

Only in human form can new Karma be created and liberation achieved.

**Predictions:**
- Human consciousness qualitatively different (self-reflection)
- Moral agency unique to humans (verified)
- Spiritual capacity unique (verified)

---

## 4.3 Null Hypotheses

### H0a: Random Evolution

Evolution is purely random mutation plus selection with no purpose or direction.

**If true:**
- No consistent direction (not observed)
- No convergence beyond physics (not true)
- Consciousness fully explained by mechanism (not achieved)

### H0b: Consciousness Emerges

Consciousness is a product of brain complexity and doesn't exist in simpler forms.

**If true:**
- Clear emergence threshold (not found)
- No consciousness in simple organisms (debated)
- Hard problem solved (not solved)

## 4.5 Falsification Criteria

### H1 would be falsified if:

1. **Evolution truly random** — No pattern, direction, or constraints
2. **Hard problem solved** — Consciousness fully explained by mechanism
3. **No consciousness gradient** — Sharp emergence threshold found
4. **Human not unique** — Animals achieve liberation
