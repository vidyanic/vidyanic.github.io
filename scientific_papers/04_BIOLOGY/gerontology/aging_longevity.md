**Telomeres:**

- Shorten with each division
- When too short ‚Üí Cell death
- The "fuse" of cellular aging

### 4.2 Telomerase

**Telomerase:**
- Enzyme that extends telomeres
- Active in stem cells, cancer
- Can be activated therapeutically
- Potential rejuvenation tool

### 4.3 Lifestyle Effects

**Telomeres are affected by:**
- Stress (shortens)
- Meditation (lengthens)
- Exercise (protects)
- Diet (influences)
