**Key Figures:**
1. **Abraham Maslow** ‚Äî Peak experiences, self-transcendence

**Hypothesis:** Transpersonal experiences are not pathological but developmental ‚Äî higher stages of human potential. They represent contact with realities beyond the ego-constructed world.
