## 11.5 Final Statement

### 11.5.1 What Freud Found

Freud discovered that most of the mind operates unconsciously, that repressed content drives behavior, and that bringing the unconscious to consciousness heals. This is the discovery of **Chitta** and the therapeutic power of **Svadhyaya** (self-study).

### 11.5.2 What Jung Extended

Jung extended this to include the collective dimension — universal patterns shared across humanity. He called these archetypes and the repository the collective unconscious. This is the discovery of **Devas** and **Hiranyagarbha** (cosmic mind).

### 11.5.3 What Yoga Completes

Yoga completes the map by:
- Identifying the **witness** (Atman) who observes the mind
- Providing **systematic methods** for Chitta stilling
- Offering **liberation** (Moksha) as ultimate goal
- Integrating psychology with **cosmic reality**

### 11.5.4 The Synthesis

**Depth psychology is Chitta Vidya rediscovered.**

The West found the basement and foundations of the psyche. The Vedic tradition has the complete architectural blueprints — from basement to rooftop to the sky beyond.

Integration is now possible. The maps can be combined. The journey can be completed.
