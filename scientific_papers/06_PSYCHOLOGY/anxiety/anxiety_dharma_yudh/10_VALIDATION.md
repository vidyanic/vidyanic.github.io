## 10.1 Mathematical Formulas

### 10.1.1 Dharma Conflict Index

```
DCI = (Paradharma_Load × Conflict_Intensity) / Svadharma_Expression

WHERE:
Paradharma_Load = Sum of inauthentic activities (0-10)
Conflict_Intensity = Emotional distress from mismatch (0-10)
Svadharma_Expression = Authentic activity proportion (0.1-1.0)

INTERPRETATION:
DCI < 2: Low anxiety risk
DCI 2-5: Moderate anxiety risk
DCI > 5: High anxiety risk
```

### 10.1.2 Purpose-Anxiety Correlation

```
r = -0.50 (pooled meta-analysis)

Anxiety = k × (1 - Purpose_Score)^α

WHERE:
k = Individual sensitivity
Purpose_Score = 0-1 (normalized)
α = ~1.5 (exponential relationship)
```

### 10.1.3 Flow-Anxiety Relationship

```
During Flow:
Anxiety = 0 (baseline)

Flow Probability:
P(Flow) = f(Skill, Challenge, Svadharma_Alignment)

Optimal: Skill ≈ Challenge AND Task = Svadharma
```
