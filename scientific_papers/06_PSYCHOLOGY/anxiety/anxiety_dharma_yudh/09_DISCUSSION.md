## 9.2 Implications for Psychology

### 9.2.1 Existential Integration

The Dharma model provides existential therapy with:
- Systematic framework (Svadharma/Paradharma)
- Clear treatment protocol (Gita model)
- Practical assessment tools
- Testable predictions

### 9.2.2 Purpose-Based Intervention

```
DHARMA DISCOVERY PROTOCOL:
==========================

1. ASSESSMENT
   • What is your natural temperament? (Varna)
   • What activities bring flow? (Svadharma signals)
   • What feels forced? (Paradharma indicators)
   
2. CLARIFICATION
   • Separate external expectations from inner calling
   • Identify Moha (delusions) about identity
   
3. ALIGNMENT
   • Small steps toward Svadharma
   • Gradual reduction of Paradharma
   
4. INTEGRATION
   • Karma Yoga (action without attachment)
   • Accept imperfect Svadharma over perfect Paradharma
```
