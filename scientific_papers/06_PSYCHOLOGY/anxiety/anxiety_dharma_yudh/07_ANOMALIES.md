## 7.1 Biological Anxiety Disorders

### 7.1.1 The Question

What about anxiety with clear biological causes (thyroid, etc.)?

### 7.1.2 Resolution

```
BIOLOGICAL FACTORS:
===================

Some anxiety has medical causes:
• Hyperthyroidism
• Caffeine/stimulant excess
• Medication side effects
• Genetic predisposition

DHARMA MODEL STILL APPLIES:
===========================
• Biological factors set THRESHOLD
• Dharma conflict determines CONTENT
• Even biologically-prone individuals show:
  - Worse symptoms when misaligned
  - Better when aligned
  
The body predisposes; the life triggers.
```

## 7.3 Successful but Anxious

### 7.3.1 The Question

Why are some "successful" people deeply anxious?

### 7.3.2 Resolution

```
SUCCESS ≠ SVADHARMA:
===================

External success often comes FROM Paradharma:
• CEO with Brahmana nature (should be teaching)
• Doctor because parents wanted (Vaishya nature)
• Lawyer for money (Shudra nature = craftwork)

THE MORE "SUCCESS":
===================
• Deeper investment in wrong path
• Harder to change
• More to lose
• MORE anxiety, not less

"What does it profit a man to gain the world and lose his soul?"
This is literal, not metaphorical.
```

## 7.4 Trauma-Based Anxiety

### 7.4.1 The Question

What about PTSD and trauma-induced anxiety?

### 7.4.2 Resolution

```
TRAUMA AND DHARMA:
==================

Trauma can:
1. REVEAL existing Dharma conflict (crisis brings it up)
2. CREATE Dharma confusion (loss of purpose after trauma)
3. BLOCK Dharma expression (fear prevents aligned action)

Treatment approach:
• Process trauma (standard approaches)
• AND restore Dharma alignment
• Both needed for full healing

Trauma therapy without Dharma = partial healing
Dharma therapy without trauma processing = premature
```

## 7.6 Medication Necessity

### 7.6.1 The Question

Aren't medications sometimes necessary?

### 7.6.2 Resolution

```
MEDICATION ROLE:
================

Medications CAN be useful:
• Acute crisis (to stabilize)
• Severe symptoms (to function)
• While doing deeper work

MEDICATIONS ARE NOT:
====================
• The solution (they're a bridge)
• Long-term cure (temporary support)
• Alternative to Dharma work

INTEGRATED APPROACH:
====================
1. Stabilize with medication if needed
2. Address Dharma conflict
3. Taper medication as conflict resolves
4. Maintain Dharma alignment
```
