## Appendix B: DSM-5 Panic Attack Criteria

A discrete period of intense fear in which ≥4 symptoms develop abruptly:

1. ✓ Palpitations, pounding heart
2. ✓ Sweating
3. ✓ Trembling or shaking
4. ✓ Shortness of breath
5. ✓ Chest pain
6. ✓ Nausea
7. ✓ Dizziness
8. ✓ Derealization/depersonalization
9. ✓ Fear of losing control
10. ✓ Fear of dying
11. ✓ Numbness/tingling
12. ✓ Chills or hot flushes

**Arjuna matches: 9/12 explicitly, others implied**
