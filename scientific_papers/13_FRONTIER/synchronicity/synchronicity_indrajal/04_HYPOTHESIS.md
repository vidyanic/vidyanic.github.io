# 4. HYPOTHESIS

---

## 4.1 Primary Hypothesis

**H1: Synchronicity is the perceivable manifestation of Indrajal — the cosmic interconnection of all consciousness and events. Meaningful coincidences occur when hidden network connections surface into awareness, amplified by Sankalpa (intention).**

---

## 4.2 Sub-Hypotheses

### H1a: Reality is Non-Locally Connected

All events and consciousnesses are connected in a network (Indrajal) beyond physical causation.

**Predictions:**
- Entanglement exists (verified in physics)
- Non-local correlations measurable (GCP data)
- Consciousness affects distant systems (PEAR data)

### H1b: Synchronicity is Network Behavior

Synchronicities are normal network operations, not anomalies.

**Predictions:**
- Everyone experiences synchronicity
- Patterns are universal
- Not random distribution

### H1c: Sankalpa Amplifies

Intention (Sankalpa) strengthens network connections and increases synchronicity.

**Predictions:**
- Focused intention -> More synchronicities
- Sattva-dominant states -> Clearer perception
- Collective intention -> Larger effects

### H1d: Meaning is Real

The "meaning" in synchronicity is not projected but inherent in the network structure.

**Predictions:**
- Similar meaning patterns cross-culturally
- Archetypes appear universally
- Meaning precedes interpretation

---

## 4.3 Null Hypotheses

### H0a: Pure Chance

Synchronicities are chance events noticed due to confirmation bias.

**If true:**
- No excess correlation in studies (contradicted by data)
- Equal distribution across attention levels (not observed)
- No replication in controlled studies (some replication exists)

### H0b: No Non-Local Connection

All correlation is local/causal; no hidden connections exist.

**If true:**
- Entanglement wouldn't exist (but it does)
- GCP data would be random (shows deviations)
- No psi effects (contested but present)

## 4.5 Falsification Criteria

### H1 would be falsified if:

1. **No non-local correlation** — All entanglement results explained locally
2. **GCP completely random** — No deviation during any global event
3. **Universal zero psi** — No replication of any psi study
4. **Synchronicity culturally specific** — No universal patterns

Current evidence does not support these null hypotheses.
