### Parental Coaching

```
AGAINST:
❌ Often against parents' beliefs
❌ Details unknown to parents
❌ Independent verification
❌ Sometimes embarrasses family
❌ Recorded before investigation
```

### Fraud

```
AGAINST:
❌ Stevenson/Tucker investigated carefully
❌ Many families disadvantaged by claims
❌ Cross-cultural occurrence
❌ Children show genuine emotional response
❌ Decades of consistent data
```

### Cryptomnesia (Hidden Memory)

```
AGAINST:
❌ No access to information demonstrated
❌ Obscure details impossible to learn
❌ Very young children (2-3 years)
❌ Verification done before any research
❌ Foreign language in some cases
```

### Genetic Memory

```
AGAINST:
❌ Previous person often not related
❌ Different families entirely
❌ How would memory transfer genetically?
❌ Birthmarks have no genetic mechanism
```

**All alternative explanations fail to account for the full evidence.**
