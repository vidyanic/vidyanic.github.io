## Appendix B: Presentiment Studies

### B.1 Summary Table

| Study | Measure | Effect | Replication |
|-------|---------|--------|-------------|

| Bierman 2000 | fMRI | Significant | Yes |
| McCraty 2004 | HRV | Significant | Yes |
| Mossbridge 2012 | Meta | Significant | 26 studies |

### B.2 Methodology

Typical presentiment study:
1. Baseline measurement
2. Random stimulus selection (after measurement)
3. Stimulus presentation
4. Compare pre-stimulus to baseline
5. Emotional vs neutral comparison
