## 1.1 The Concept of Rta (ऋत)

In the Backend Framework, the universe is not a chaotic collection of random events. It is a highly optimized simulation governed by **Rta** — the primordial cosmic order.

If the universe is a simulation, Rta represents the **kernel-level constants** and **invariants** that cannot be modified from within the system.

### 1.1.1 Why Laws Exist
From a systems engineering perspective, laws exist to:
1. **Ensure Consistency:** So that interaction between different "shards" or "instances" of reality results in predictable outcomes.
2. **Optimize Rendering:** By having fixed rules (like the speed of light), the engine can skip unnecessary calculations.
3. **Establish a Playing Field:** For the Jiva (player) to have a meaningful experience, the rules must be stable.
