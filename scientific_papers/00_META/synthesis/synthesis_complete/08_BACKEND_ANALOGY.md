**Game:** Maximum polygon count, frame rate.  
**Reality:** Planck scale, speed of light.  
**Match:** ✅ Perfect

### 8.4.3 Consistent Rules

**Game:** Physics rules are hardcoded.

**Match:** ✅ Perfect

### 8.4.4 Respawn

**Game:** Die, respawn with some progress.

**Match:** ✅ Perfect

**Game:** Developers can break rules.
