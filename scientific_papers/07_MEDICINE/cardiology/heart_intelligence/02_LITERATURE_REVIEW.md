## 2.1 NEUROCARDIOLOGY

### The Discovery of Heart Neurons

**J. Andrew Armour (1991):**
- Discovered intrinsic cardiac nervous system
- Named it "heart brain"
- ~40,000 neurons in heart
- Can function independently

**Key Paper:**
> Armour, J.A. (1991). "Intrinsic cardiac neurons." *Journal of Cardiovascular Electrophysiology*, 2(4), 331-341.

### Heart-Brain Pathway Research

**Lacey & Lacey (1978):**
- Heart-brain communication is bidirectional
- Heart affects perception and cognition
- Cardiac deceleration increases attention

## 2.3 ELECTROMAGNETIC FIELD

### Measurement Studies

**McCraty et al. (2009):**
- Heart's EM field 60x stronger than brain's
- Measurable several feet from body
- Contains encoded information
- Changes with emotional state

**Key Paper:**
> McCraty, R. (2009). "The Energetic Heart: Bioelectromagnetic Interactions Within and Between People." *HeartMath Research Center*.
