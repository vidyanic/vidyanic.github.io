## 3.7 THE FORMULA

### Placebo/Sankalpa Effectiveness

```
EFFECTIVENESS = f(Intention, Belief, Ritual, Context, Authority)

Mathematically:

E = I × B × R × C × A

WHERE:
I = Intention strength (0-1)
B = Belief/faith level (0-1)
R = Ritual engagement (0-1)
C = Context appropriateness (0-1)
A = Authority factor (1.0 - 2.0)

EXAMPLE:
Strong intention (0.8) × 
Strong belief (0.8) ×
Good ritual (0.7) ×
Proper context (0.9) ×
High authority (1.5)
= 0.8 × 0.8 × 0.7 × 0.9 × 1.5
= 0.60 (60% effectiveness)
```
