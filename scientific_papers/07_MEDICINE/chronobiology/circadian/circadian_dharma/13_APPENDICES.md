## APPENDIX A: COMPLETE DINACHARYA PROTOCOL

### A.1 Traditional Dinacharya

```
COMPLETE DAILY ROUTINE:
=======================

BRAHMA MUHURTA (3:30-6 AM):
• Wake 96 minutes before sunrise
• Attend natural urges
• Wash face, mouth
• Drink warm water
• Eliminate bowels
• Brush teeth, tongue scraping
• Oil pulling (Gandusha)
• Eye wash
• Meditation/Prayer

MORNING (6-10 AM):
• Abhyanga (oil massage)
• Exercise (Vyayama)
• Bath
• Pranayama
• Light breakfast
• Begin work

MIDDAY (10 AM-2 PM):
• Peak work period
• Main meal (12-1 PM)
• Short rest after eating
• Continue work

AFTERNOON (2-6 PM):
• Creative work
• Light snack if needed
• Exercise if not morning
• Complete day's work

EVENING (6-10 PM):
• Light dinner (6-7 PM)
• Leisure, family time
• Wind down activities
• Foot massage
• Brief meditation
• Sleep by 10 PM

NIGHT (10 PM-6 AM):
• Deep sleep
• Repair and regeneration
• Dreams (later part)
• Natural awakening
```

## APPENDIX D: LIGHT EXPOSURE GUIDELINES

### D.1 Optimal Light Hygiene

| Time | Light Recommendation |
|------|---------------------|
| **Morning (first 30 min)** | Bright natural light (10,000+ lux) |
| **Daytime** | Bright environment |
| **After sunset** | Dim warm light (<200 lux) |
| **2 hours before bed** | No blue light, very dim |
| **Bedroom** | Complete darkness |

### D.2 Light Sources

| Source | Lux Level | Use |
|--------|-----------|-----|
| Sunlight | 10,000-100,000 | Morning entrainment |
| Overcast | 1,000-10,000 | Still beneficial |
| Office light | 300-500 | Insufficient |
| Home light | 50-200 | Evening only |
| Screen | 100-300 (blue) | Avoid at night |
| Candle | 10-15 | Evening appropriate |

## APPENDIX F: SLEEP OPTIMIZATION

### F.1 Pre-Sleep Routine

```
2 HOURS BEFORE BED:
===================

• Dim lights
• No screens (or blue light blockers)
• No heavy food
• No stimulating content
• Light activities only
• Begin winding down

30 MINUTES BEFORE:
==================

• Foot massage with oil
• Brief meditation
• Gratitude practice
• Comfortable pajamas
• Cool, dark room
• Consistent bedtime
```

### F.2 Sleep Environment

| Factor | Optimal Condition |
|--------|------------------|
| Temperature | 60-67°F (16-19°C) |
| Light | Complete darkness |
| Sound | Quiet or white noise |
| Air | Fresh, well-ventilated |
| Bed | Comfortable, clean |
| Electronics | None in bedroom |
