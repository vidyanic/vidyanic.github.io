## 7.1 Lunar Rhythm Debate

### 7.1.1 The Question

Do lunar cycles affect biology?
- Traditional: Yes (Tithi, Paksha)
- Modern: Debated

### 7.1.2 Evidence

**Supporting:**
- Menstrual cycle ~28 days (similar to lunar)
- Some sleep studies show moon effects
- Marine life synchronized to moon

**Against:**
- Human menstrual cycle varies (not exactly lunar)
- Many studies show no moon effect
- Confirmation bias possible

**Status:** Possible but not proven
