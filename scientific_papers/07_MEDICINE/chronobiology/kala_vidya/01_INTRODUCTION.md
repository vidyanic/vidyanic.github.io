## 1.1 The Discovery of Body Clocks

### 1.1.1 Nobel Prize 2017

Jeffrey Hall, Michael Rosbash, and Michael Young won Nobel Prize for discovering molecular mechanisms controlling circadian rhythms:
- Clock genes (CLOCK, BMAL1, PER, CRY)
- Negative feedback loops
- ~24-hour autonomous oscillation
- Universal across life forms

### 1.1.2 Chronobiology Established

Modern chronobiology demonstrates:
- Circadian (day-night) rhythms
- Ultradian (shorter) cycles
- Infradian (longer) cycles
- Seasonal and annual patterns
