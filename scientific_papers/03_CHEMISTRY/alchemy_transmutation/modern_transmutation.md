## 2. Nuclear Transmutation (Proven)

### 2.1 The Discovery

**Rutherford (1917):**
- First artificial transmutation
- Nitrogen -> Oxygen via alpha particles
- Elements CAN change!

### 2.2 How It Works

```
NUCLEAR TRANSMUTATION:
=====================

NUCLEUS (Protons + Neutrons)
    |
    v Add/remove protons
    
DIFFERENT ELEMENT!

14N + 4He -> 17O + 1H
(Nitrogen + Alpha -> Oxygen + Proton)
```

### 2.3 Modern Examples

| From | To | Method |
|------|-----|--------|
| Mercury | Gold | Particle accelerator |
| Bismuth | Gold | Neutron bombardment |
| Lead | Gold | Nuclear reaction |
| Uranium | Plutonium | Neutron absorption |

**Transmutation IS possible. Alchemists were right in principle.**

## 3. LENR: Low Energy Nuclear Reactions

### 3.1 What Is LENR?

**Controversial but documented:**
- Nuclear reactions at low temperatures
- "Cold fusion" related
- Element changes with less energy than expected
- Ongoing research

### 3.2 Evidence

**Key experiments:**
- Fleischmann-Pons (1989) — Initial claim
- Subsequent replications (some successful)
- Japanese, Italian, US research
- Transmutation products detected

### 3.3 The Mechanism (Proposed)

**May involve:**
- Quantum tunneling
- Electron screening
- Lattice effects
- Unknown physics

**Alchemists may have accessed similar processes.**
