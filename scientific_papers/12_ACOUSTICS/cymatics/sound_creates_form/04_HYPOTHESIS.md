# 04. Hypothesis: Formal Claims

## 4.1 Primary Hypothesis

### H1: Sound Creates Form

**Statement:** Sound frequencies create specific, reproducible geometric forms in physical media, demonstrating that vibration is formative.

**Formal Expression:**
```
Let f = frequency (Hz)
Let P = pattern (geometry)

H1: For each f, there exists a unique P such that f -> P
    And: f1 = f2 implies P1 = P2 (reproducibility)
```

**Testable Predictions:**
- Applying frequency f to any Chladni plate produces pattern P
- This holds for any experimenter, location, time
- Relationship is deterministic, not random

## 4.2 Secondary Hypotheses

### H2: Vedic Correspondence

**Statement:** The observable relationship between sound and form in cymatics validates the Vedic principle of Shabda -> Rupa (Sound -> Form).

**Evidence Required:**
- Vedic texts claim sound creates form
- Cymatics demonstrates sound creates form
- The mechanisms align conceptually

### H3: Biological Template

**Statement:** Natural biological forms may be standing wave patterns of vibration at the cellular/molecular level.

**Supporting Observations:**
- Cymatic patterns resemble natural forms
- Cells, crystals, shells show similar geometry
- Morphogenetic fields may be vibrational

### H4: Sound Healing Mechanism

**Statement:** Sound can influence biological systems because form is fundamentally vibrational, and changing frequency changes form.

**Implications:**
- Therapeutic sound is not placebo
- Specific frequencies have specific effects
- Ancient sound healing has mechanistic basis

## 4.3 Null Hypotheses

### H0-1: Pattern is Random

**Statement:** Cymatic patterns are random coincidences, not deterministic.

**Counter-Evidence:**
- Same frequency = same pattern (always)
- Reproducibility across all conditions
- Predictable by physics

### H0-2: No Vedic Connection

**Statement:** Correspondence with Vedic cosmology is superficial.

**Counter-Argument:**
- Vedas specifically claim sound creates form
- Cymatics specifically shows sound creates form
- The match is not analogy but identity
