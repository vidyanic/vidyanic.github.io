## 3.2 Physics of Cymatics

### 3.2.1 Standing Waves

**Mechanism:**
When a vibrating plate is excited at specific frequencies, standing waves form. These waves have:
- **Nodes:** Points of no movement
- **Antinodes:** Points of maximum movement

Particles (sand, water) collect at nodes, making the wave pattern visible.

### 3.2.2 Mode Shapes

Each frequency produces a characteristic "mode shape" — a specific pattern of nodes and antinodes. This is determined by:
- Frequency
- Plate/medium geometry
- Material properties

### 3.2.3 Resonance

At resonant frequencies, the pattern is most pronounced. This aligns with the Vedic concept that each entity has a natural frequency (svara) at which it most fully expresses.
