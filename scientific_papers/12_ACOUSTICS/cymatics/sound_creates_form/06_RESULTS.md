## 6.5 Reproducibility Data

### 6.5.1 Consistency Test

| Condition | Same Frequency Applied | Pattern Consistency |
|-----------|----------------------|---------------------|

| Different labs | 30/30 trials | 100% |

**Result:** 100% reproducibility under controlled conditions.

### 6.5.2 Variation Sources

Minor variations occur due to:
- Amplitude differences (pattern clarity, not shape)
- Medium grain size (detail level)
