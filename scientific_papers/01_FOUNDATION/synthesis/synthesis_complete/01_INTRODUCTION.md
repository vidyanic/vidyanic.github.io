## 1.1 The Problem of Fragmentation

Modern knowledge is fragmented:

| Domain | Asks | Ignores |
|--------|------|---------|
| Physics | How does matter behave? | Why does observation matter? |
| Biology | How does life work? | What makes it "alive"? |
| Psychology | How does mind function? | What IS consciousness? |
| Philosophy | What is real? | How to verify? |
| Spirituality | How to transcend? | Scientific validation |

**Each domain sees a piece. None sees the whole.**

**Reality is consciousness (Brahman) rendering itself through a projection system (Maya), creating the appearance of a differentiated world (Jagat) in which individuated consciousness (Jiva) experiences, learns, and can ultimately recognize its true nature (Moksha).**
