## 9.6 Objections and Responses

### 9.6.1 "This is just idealism — unscientific"

**Response:**
- Idealism is a metaphysical position, not anti-scientific
- We use scientific evidence (anomalies)
- Materialism also has metaphysical assumptions
- The question is: Which metaphysics fits evidence?

### 9.6.2 "NDEs could be brain artifacts"

**Response:**
- Some verified during flat EEG
- Veridical perceptions difficult to explain as artifacts
- Coherent narratives unlike brain malfunction
- Even if some are artifacts, the pattern suggests more

### 9.6.3 "This makes science impossible"

**Response:**
- Science studies Prakriti (measurable phenomena)
- Brain science continues — studying correlates
- Physics continues — studying matter
- Only consciousness-generation claim changes

### 9.6.4 "If consciousness is everywhere, why don't rocks experience?"

**Response:**
- Possibly they do (panpsychism)
- Or: Consciousness requires certain structures to manifest locally
- Or: We can't know — but that's the receiver model, not generator
