## Abstract

**The Conventional View:** The brain GENERATES consciousness. Neurons create experience. Mind is what brain does.

**The Alternative:** The brain RECEIVES consciousness. Like a radio receiving broadcast signals, the brain transduces non-physical consciousness into physical experience.

**Evidence:**
1. Brain damage changes experience (consistent with receiver damage)
2. NDEs suggest consciousness during brain shutdown
3. Psychedelics reduce brain activity while expanding experience
4. Savant abilities suggest access to larger information field

**Hypothesis:** The brain is an interface between non-physical consciousness and physical body, not the source of consciousness itself.

**Keywords:** Brain, consciousness, receiver, transducer, filter, interface, neuroscience

## 3. Evidence for Receiver Model

### 3.1 Near-Death Experiences

**NDEs occur during:**
- Cardiac arrest (no blood to brain)
- Deep anesthesia
- Clinical death

**Features:**
- Vivid experiences
- Often veridical (verified accurate perceptions)
- Life review, tunnel, light
- Profound meaning

**Problem for materialists:**
- How can brain produce experience when non-functional?
- Receiver model: Consciousness continues, receiver off

### 3.2 Psychedelics and "Reducing Brain Activity"

**Counter-intuitive finding:**
- Psychedelics (psilocybin) REDUCE brain activity
- But EXPAND subjective experience

**Carhart-Harris et al.:**
- fMRI shows reduced activity in default mode network
- Subjects report expanded, profound experiences

**Receiver interpretation:**
- Less filtering = More consciousness comes through
- Brain normally CONSTRAINS consciousness
- Reducing brain activity releases constraints

### 3.3 Terminal Lucidity

**Phenomenon:**
- Dementia patients suddenly lucid before death
- After years of confusion
- Clear memory, recognition, communication
- Dies shortly after

**Problem for materialists:**
- Damaged brain shouldn't suddenly work
- No time for repair

**Receiver interpretation:**
- Approaching death, filtering decreases
- Consciousness flows more freely
- "Radio" about to be discarded, letting more through

### 3.4 Savant Abilities

**Acquired savants:**
- Brain injury â†’ Sudden abilities
- Calendar calculation, art, music
- Abilities not learned

**Problem:**
- How does damage CREATE ability?

**Receiver interpretation:**
- Damage to filter reveals access to larger field
- Information already exists; brain was blocking
