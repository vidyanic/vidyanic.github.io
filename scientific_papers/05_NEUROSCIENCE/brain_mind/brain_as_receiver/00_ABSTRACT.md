## Research Question

Does the brain **generate** consciousness, or **receive/filter** it?

---

## Background

The dominant scientific paradigm holds that the brain produces consciousness ‚Äî that neurons firing somehow create subjective experience. This "generator model" has failed to explain the explanatory gap: how physical processes become subjective feelings.

An alternative model, proposed by William James (1898) and supported by Backend Architecture documentation, suggests the brain is a **receiver and filter** of consciousness, not its generator.

## Methodology

We evaluate four categories of evidence:

1. **Near-Death Experiences (NDEs):** Consciousness during brain shutdown
2. **Psychedelic Research:** Reduced brain activity with expanded experience
3. **Terminal Lucidity:** Sudden clarity in dementia patients before death
4. **Savant Syndrome:** Brain damage revealing hidden abilities
