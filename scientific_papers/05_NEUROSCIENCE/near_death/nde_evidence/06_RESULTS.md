## 6.1 Prospective Study Results

### 6.1.1 Van Lommel Study (Lancet, 2001)

**The Gold Standard**

| Finding | Value | Significance |
|---------|-------|--------------|
| **Sample** | 344 cardiac arrest survivors | Largest prospective |
| **NDE incidence** | 18% | Much higher than expected |
| **Deep NDEs** | 12% | Profound experiences |
| **OBE** | 24% of NDEs | Quarter left body |
| **Light** | 23% | Common feature |
| **Deceased** | 32% | Met dead relatives |
| **Life review** | 13% | Panoramic memory |

**Critical Findings:**

1. **No medical predictor of NDE**
   - Duration of arrest: No correlation
   - Medication: No correlation
   - Blood gases: No correlation

2. **Veridical cases documented**
   - Denture case: Nurse removed dentures; patient described accurately
   - Multiple accurate perceptions verified

3. **Long-term effects**
   - 2-year follow-up: Lasting changes
   - 8-year follow-up: Transformation persists

## 6.4 Alternative Explanations Fail

### 6.4.1 Hypoxia

**Claim:** Oxygen deprivation causes NDE-like experiences

**Evidence Against:**
| Observation | Significance |
|-------------|--------------|
| NDEs occur in non-hypoxic conditions | Not required |
| Hypoxia causes confusion, not clarity | Wrong effect |
| Fighter pilot blackout (Whinnery) differs | Different experience |
| Van Lommel: Blood gases same in NDE vs non-NDE | No correlation |

### 6.4.2 Drugs

**Claim:** Endogenous (endorphins, DMT) or exogenous drugs cause NDEs

**Evidence Against:**
| Observation | Significance |
|-------------|--------------|
| NDEs occur without drugs | Not required |
| Drug experiences differ qualitatively | Different character |
| Van Lommel: Medication no predictor | No correlation |
| Verified perceptions during drug-free arrests | Can't be drug |

### 6.4.3 REM Intrusion

**Claim:** NDEs are REM sleep intruding into consciousness

**Evidence Against:**
| Observation | Significance |
|-------------|--------------|
| REM requires brain activity | Brain is flat |
| Dreams are not veridical | NDEs include verified perception |
| Dream quality differs | NDEs more real than real |

### 6.4.4 False Memory

**Claim:** NDEs are constructed after revival

**Evidence Against:**
| Observation | Significance |
|-------------|--------------|
| Veridical perceptions verified | Can't construct accurate details |
| Immediate reports match later reports | Consistent memory |
| Children report before exposure to NDE media | Not culturally learned |
