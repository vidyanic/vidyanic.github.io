## APPENDIX A: SLEEP STAGE SPECIFICATIONS

### A.1 EEG Characteristics

| Stage | Dominant Waves | Frequency | Amplitude | Description |
|-------|---------------|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| Wake (eyes open) | Beta | 13-30 Hz | Low | Desynchronized |
| Wake (eyes closed) | Alpha | 8-12 Hz | Moderate | Relaxed, rhythmic |
| N1 | Theta | 4-7 Hz | Low | Transition |
| N2 | Theta + K/spindles | 4-7 Hz | Variable | True sleep |
| N3 | Delta | 0.5-4 Hz | High | Deep sleep |
| REM | Mixed (beta-like) | Variable | Low | Desynchronized |

### A.2 Physiological Markers

| Stage | Heart Rate | Breathing | Muscle Tone | Eye Movement |
|-------|------------|-----------|-------------|--------------|
| Wake | Variable | Controlled | Active | Voluntary |
| N1 | Slowing | Slowing | Reduced | Slow rolling |
| N2 | Slow, regular | Slow, regular | Low | None |
| N3 | Slowest | Slow, deep | Very low | None |
| REM | Variable | Irregular | Atonia | Rapid |

## APPENDIX B: MANDUKYA UPANISHAD KEY VERSES

### B.1 The Four States (Verses 3-7)

**Verse 3 (Jagrat):**
```
जागरितस्थानो बहिष्प्रज्ञः सप्ताङ्गः एकोनविंशतिमुखः
स्थूलभुग्वैश्वानरः प्रथमः पादः ।

"The first quarter is Vaishvanara (Universal Man),
whose sphere is the waking state,
who cognizes external objects,
who has seven limbs and nineteen mouths,
and who enjoys gross objects."
```

**Verse 4 (Swapna):**
```
स्वप्नस्थानोऽन्तःप्रज्ञः सप्ताङ्गः एकोनविंशतिमुखः
प्रविविक्तभुक्तैजसो द्वितीयः पादः ।

"The second quarter is Taijasa (Luminous),
whose sphere is the dream state,
who cognizes internal objects,
who has seven limbs and nineteen mouths,
and who enjoys subtle objects."
```

**Verse 5 (Sushupti):**
```
यत्र सुप्तो न कंचन कामं कामयते
न कंचन स्वप्नं पश्यति तत्सुषुप्तम् ।
सुषुप्तस्थान एकीभूतः प्रज्ञानघन एवानन्दमयो ह्यानन्दभुक्चेतोमुखः
प्राज्ञस्तृतीयः पादः ।

"That is deep sleep where the sleeper
desires nothing and dreams nothing.
The third quarter is Prajna (Wisdom),
whose sphere is deep sleep,
who has become one mass of consciousness,
who is full of bliss."
```

**Verse 7 (Turiya):**
```
नान्तःप्रज्ञं न बहिष्प्रज्ञं नोभयतःप्रज्ञं
न प्रज्ञानघनं न प्रज्ञं नाप्रज्ञम् ।
अदृश्यमव्यवहार्यमग्राह्यमलक्षणमचिन्त्यमव्यपदेश्यम्
एकात्मप्रत्ययसारं प्रपञ्चोपशमं शान्तं शिवमद्वैतं
चतुर्थं मन्यन्ते स आत्मा स विज्ञेयः ।

"That which is not conscious of the internal world,
nor conscious of the external world,
nor conscious of both worlds,
nor a mass of consciousness,
neither knowing nor unknowing —
invisible, ungraspable, without marks,
unthinkable, unnamable,
the essence of Self-knowledge,
wherein all phenomena cease,
peaceful, blissful, non-dual —
that is the Fourth (Turiya). That is the Self.
That is to be known."
```

## APPENDIX C: LUCID DREAMING TECHNIQUES

### C.1 Induction Methods

| Technique | Description | Effectiveness |
|-----------|-------------|---------------|
| **MILD** | Mnemonic Induction | Set intention before sleep |
| **WILD** | Wake-Initiated | Enter dream consciously |
| **WBTB** | Wake-Back-To-Bed | Wake, return to sleep |
| **Reality Testing** | Question reality | Throughout day |
| **FILD** | Finger-Induced | Subtle movement focus |

### C.2 Reality Checks

- Look at hands (distorted in dreams)
- Read text twice (changes in dreams)
- Push finger through palm
- Check time twice
- Try to fly or float

## APPENDIX D: YOGA NIDRA STAGES

### D.1 Traditional Sequence

```
YOGA NIDRA PRACTICE STAGES:
===========================

1. PREPARATION
   • Lie in Shavasana
   • Settle the body
   • Set Sankalpa (intention)

2. ROTATION OF CONSCIOUSNESS
   • Systematic body awareness
   • Right side, left side, back, front
   • Pratyahara (sense withdrawal)

3. BREATH AWARENESS
   • Natural breath observation
   • Counting (optional)
   • Deepening relaxation

4. OPPOSITES
   • Heavy/light, hot/cold
   • Pleasure/pain
   • Expansion/contraction

5. VISUALIZATION
   • Guided imagery
   • Symbolic content
   • Archetypal scenes

6. SANKALPA
   • Repeat intention
   • Plant in subconscious

7. RETURN
   • Gradual externalization
   • Body awareness
   • Gentle awakening
```

## APPENDIX F: SLEEP HYGIENE GUIDELINES

### F.1 Optimal Practices

```
BEFORE SLEEP:
=============
• No screens 1 hour before
• Dim lights
• Cool room (65-68°F / 18-20°C)
• No caffeine after 2pm
• Light dinner
• Meditation/relaxation practice
• Consistent bedtime

DURING SLEEP:
=============
• Dark room
• Quiet or white noise
• Comfortable bedding
• No disruptions

MORNING:
========
• Consistent wake time
• Sunlight exposure
• Recall dreams before moving
• Gentle transition
```
