## 5.9 LIMITATIONS

### Acknowledged Constraints

1. **Turiya is hard to measure**
   - By definition, it's the measurer
   - Self-referential problem

2. **Subjective reports**
   - Dream content is self-reported
   - Deep sleep experience is inferred

3. **Advanced practitioners are rare**
   - Yoga Nidra masters are few
   - Small sample sizes

4. **Philosophical interpretation**
   - Mandukya requires interpretation
   - Multiple valid readings
