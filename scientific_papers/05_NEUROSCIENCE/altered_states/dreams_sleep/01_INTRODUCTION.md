## 1.1 THE SLEEP MYSTERY

Every night, humans spend 6-8 hours in an altered state of consciousness. We lose awareness of the external world, yet something persists — we wake up as "ourselves."

### The Fundamental Questions

```
SLEEP RAISES PROFOUND QUESTIONS:
===============================

1. WHERE does consciousness "go" during sleep?
2. WHY do we dream? What are dreams FOR?
3. WHAT persists in deep sleep when brain activity is minimal?
4. HOW do we wake as the same "person"?

These are not just biological questions —
they are CONSCIOUSNESS questions.
```

## 1.2 THE SCIENTIFIC VIEW

### Modern Sleep Research

| Era | Contribution |
|-----|--------------|
| 1950s | REM sleep discovered (Aserinsky, Kleitman) |
| 1960s | Sleep stages characterized (EEG) |
| 1970s | Sleep cycle architecture mapped |
| 2000s | Lucid dreaming validated scientifically |
| 2010s | Glymphatic system discovered (brain cleaning) |

### What Science Knows

- Sleep consists of distinct stages with measurable brain activity
- Dreams occur primarily in REM but also in NREM
- Sleep is essential for memory, learning, and health
- Brain "cleans" during deep sleep (glymphatic system)

### What Science Doesn't Explain

- WHY consciousness varies with sleep stage
- WHAT awareness exists in deep sleep
- Whether brain GENERATES or RECEIVES consciousness during sleep

## 1.3 THE VEDIC VIEW

### Mandukya Upanishad (3000+ years ago)

The Mandukya describes FOUR states of consciousness:

| State | Sanskrit | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| **Waking** | Jagrat (जाग्रत्) | External awareness |
| **Dreaming** | Swapna (स्वप्न) | Internal imagery |
| **Deep Sleep** | Sushupti (सुषुप्ति) | Undifferentiated bliss |
| **Fourth** | Turiya (तुरीय) | Pure witness |

### The Key Insight

```
TURIYA IS NOT A FOURTH "STATE":
===============================

Turiya is the WITNESS of all three states.

• In waking: Turiya witnesses waking
• In dreaming: Turiya witnesses dreams
• In deep sleep: Turiya witnesses... nothing?

Yet YOU wake up as "you" — SOMETHING persisted.
That something is Turiya.
```
