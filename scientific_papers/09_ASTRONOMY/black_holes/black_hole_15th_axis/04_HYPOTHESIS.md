## 4.3 NULL HYPOTHESES

**H₀₁:** Black holes are purely physical phenomena with no computational function.

**H₀₂:** The 15th axis concept is purely metaphorical with no physical correspondence.

**H₀₃:** Information is truly destroyed at singularities.

**H₀₄:** Fractal pattern is coincidental, not fundamental.

---

## 4.4 FALSIFICATION CRITERIA

The 15th axis hypothesis would be falsified if:

1. **No information preservation** — Future physics proves information truly destroyed
2. **No structural correspondence** — Black hole properties don't match 15th axis
3. **Pattern breaks** — Some scale lacks central processing point
4. **Alternative explains better** — Simpler model accounts for all data
