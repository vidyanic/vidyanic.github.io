## 3.1 Self-Regulation Mechanisms

### 3.1.1 Feedback Loops

```
EARTH'S FEEDBACK SYSTEMS:
=========================

NEGATIVE FEEDBACK (Stabilizing):
Temperature ↑ → Weathering ↑ → CO₂ ↓ → Temperature ↓
[DAISYWORLD MODEL]

BIOLOGICAL REGULATION:
O₂ production → Enables aerobic life → Life modifies atmosphere → O₂ maintained

OCEAN CHEMISTRY:
Salt input → Biological uptake → Sediment deposition → Salinity stable
```

### 3.1.2 Daisyworld Model

Lovelock's thought experiment:
- Planet with black and white daisies
- Black daisies warm (absorb light)
- White daisies cool (reflect light)
- Temperature self-regulates through competition
- Demonstrates how life can regulate climate
