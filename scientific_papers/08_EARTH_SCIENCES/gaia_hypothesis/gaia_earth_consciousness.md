## 2. Evidence for Self-Regulation

### 2.1 Atmospheric Oxygen

**The puzzle:**
- Oxygen is reactive (should disappear)
- Maintained at 21% for ~300 million years
- Too low ‚Üí No fire, limited respiration
- Too high ‚Üí Everything burns

**The regulation:**
- Photosynthesis produces O‚ÇÇ
- Respiration/decomposition consumes
- Perfect balance maintained
- Not by chance

### 2.2 Ocean Salinity

**The puzzle:**
- Rivers continuously add salt
- Oceans should get saltier
- But stable at 3.5%

**The regulation:**
- Salt deposited in sediments
- Biological processes remove
- Geological recycling
- Self-maintaining

### 2.3 Temperature

**The puzzle:**
- Sun 30% brighter than 4 billion years ago
- Earth should have heated
- But temperature relatively stable

**The regulation:**
- Albedo (reflectivity) changes
- CO‚ÇÇ weathering feedback
- Cloud formation
- Biosphere adjusts

### 2.4 CLAW Hypothesis

**Dimethyl sulfide (DMS):**
- Produced by marine organisms
- Creates cloud condensation nuclei
- Increases cloud cover
- Cools Earth when warm
- Reduces when cool
- Temperature thermostat!
