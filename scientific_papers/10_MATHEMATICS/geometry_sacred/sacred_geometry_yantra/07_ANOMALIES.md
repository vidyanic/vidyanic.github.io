# 07. Anomalies: Open Questions

## 7.1 Phi Overreach

### 7.1.1 The Problem

**Some claims about φ are exaggerated:**
- Not every ratio in the human body is φ
- Not all "golden ratio" art actually uses φ
- Some measurements are forced to fit

### 7.1.2 Legitimate vs. Forced

| Legitimate φ | Forced φ |
|--------------|----------|
| Nautilus shell (measurable) | "The Mona Lisa uses φ" (debatable) |
| Phyllotaxis (countable) | "Pyramids encode φ" (some) |
| DNA proportions (measured) | Random nature photos |

### 7.1.3 Resolution

**Distinguish between:**
- Verified φ occurrences (measurable, consistent)
- Approximate φ (close but not exact)
- Confirmation bias (seeing what we want)

**Our paper only claims verified occurrences.**

## 7.2 Why φ?

### 7.2.1 The Deeper Question

We know φ appears. But WHY is it the optimization constant?

**Possible explanations:**
1. **Physical necessity:** φ is the most efficient packing/growth
2. **Mathematical inevitability:** Self-reference leads to φ
3. **Consciousness preference:** Observer bias toward φ
4. **Rendering constant:** φ is built into reality's code

### 7.2.2 Unresolved

We can describe WHERE φ appears but not definitively WHY it's 1.618... and not some other number.
