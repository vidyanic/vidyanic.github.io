## 5.3 HISTORICAL ANALYSIS

### Revolution Comparison Method

```
FOR EACH REVOLUTION:
====================

1. COLLECT:
   • Pre-revolution employment
   • Post-revolution employment
   • Job categories before/after
   • Predictions made at the time

2. COMPARE:
   • Net change in employment
   • Types of jobs created/destroyed
   • Transition period length
   • Prediction accuracy

3. PATTERN:
   • Identify consistent pattern
   • Note variations
   • Extract principles
```
