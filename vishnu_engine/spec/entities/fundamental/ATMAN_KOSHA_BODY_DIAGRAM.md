## ‚òØÔ∏è DEATH SEQUENCE THROUGH THE KOSHAS

```

=======================================================================
‚Ä¢ Atman (consciousness) begins withdrawing from Anandamaya Kosha
‚Ä¢ Subtle decay starts (not visible externally)

‚Ä¢ Anandamaya Kosha (bliss sheath) thinning

‚Ä¢ Vijnanamaya Kosha (wisdom) collapses

‚Ä¢ Manomaya Kosha (mind) fragments
  -> Memories fade, mental coherence lost
‚Ä¢ Pranamaya Kosha (Prana) destabilizing
  -> Internal sun dimming, energy dropping
‚Ä¢ Annamaya Kosha (physical) still intact
  -> Body looks "fine" from outside

=======================================================================
‚Ä¢ Last breath = Prana exits Pranamaya Kosha
‚Ä¢ Internal sun goes dark (0% power)
‚Ä¢ Pranamaya Kosha collapses completely
‚Ä¢ Heart stops, brain stops
‚Ä¢ Clinical death occurs

PHASE 4: ANNAMAYA DECAY (After death)
=======================================================================
‚Ä¢ Physical body (Annamaya Kosha) now empty shell
‚Ä¢ Returns to Pancha Mahabhutas (5 elements)
‚Ä¢ Decomposition begins
‚Ä¢ Matter recycled

KEY INSIGHT:
‚Ä¢ Atman never dies (withdraws FIRST, days before)
‚Ä¢ Prana exits at death moment (last breath)
‚Ä¢ Physical body decays last (hours/days after)
```
