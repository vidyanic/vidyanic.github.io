## 3. Daily Death Meditation {#3-daily}

### Maranasati Practice (‡§Æ‡§∞‡§£‡§∏‡•ç‡§Æ‡•É‡§§‡§ø)

```
TIME: Evening, before sleep (10-15 minutes)
PLACE: Quiet, dark room
POSITION: Lying on back (like a corpse)

STEP 1: RELAXATION (2 min)
--------------------------
‚Ä¢ Lie in Shavasana (corpse pose)
‚Ä¢ Let all muscles release
‚Ä¢ Breathe naturally
‚Ä¢ Imagine body becoming heavy

STEP 2: ELEMENT DISSOLUTION VISUALIZATION (5 min)
-------------------------------------------------
Imagine each element dissolving:

EARTH:
"The solid parts of my body ‚Äî bones, muscles ‚Äî are dissolving.
I feel heavy, sinking into the earth.
Earth returns to earth."

WATER:
"The fluids in my body ‚Äî blood, saliva ‚Äî are drying up.
I feel thirsty, parched.
Water returns to water."

FIRE:
"The warmth in my body is withdrawing.
Feet become cold, then legs, then hands.
Fire returns to fire."

AIR:
"My breath becomes shallow, irregular.
Long gaps between breaths.
The last breath comes... and goes.
Air returns to air."

SPACE:
"All sensation disappears.
No sight, no sound, no touch.
Only awareness remains.
Space dissolves into awareness."

STEP 3: WITNESS RECOGNITION (3 min)
-----------------------------------
"The body has dissolved.
The elements have returned.
Yet I AM still here.
Who is this 'I' that remains?
This awareness that watched the dissolution?
THIS is what I truly am.
THIS never dies.
THIS is Atman."

STEP 4: CROWN EXIT VISUALIZATION (2 min)
----------------------------------------
"I gather my awareness at the heart.
I draw it upward through the Sushumna.
It rises to the crown ‚Äî the Brahma-Randhra.
Light awaits above.
I am ready to exit through the crown.
I am consciousness, not the body."

STEP 5: RETURN (2 min)
----------------------
"For now, I return to this body.
But I have practiced.
When the actual moment comes, I will know.
I will exit consciously.
I am not afraid of death.
Death is just a doorway.
I am the deathless awareness."

Slowly wiggle fingers and toes.
Take three deep breaths.
Open eyes.
```

### Weekly Intensive Practice

```
ONCE A WEEK: Full Corpse Meditation (30 min)
============================================

1. Lie in complete stillness for 20 minutes
2. Practice "I am dead" ‚Äî no movement at all
3. Observe body sensations fade
4. Identify with the witness, not the body
5. At end, "resurrect" ‚Äî recognize you were never dead
```
