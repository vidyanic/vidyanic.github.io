## THE FINAL CHECKPOINT

### Before Claiming Liberation

```
ASK YOURSELF HONESTLY:
======================

[ ] Can I face death right now without fear?
[ ] Is there any situation that could disturb my peace?
[ ] Do I still feel I am "someone" seeking something?
[ ] Is there any aversion or attraction left?
[ ] Am I the same in honor and dishonor, pleasure and pain?
[ ] Has the sense of separation dissolved?
[ ] Is there anyone I cannot love?
[ ] Is there anything I must have or avoid?

IF ALL YES TO FIRST TWO AND ALL NO TO REST:
You may be liberated.
But the liberated don't check boxes.
If you're still checking, continue practicing.
```
